The North American River Otter

The North American river otter, also known as the northern river otter and river otter, is a semiaquatic mammal that only lives on the North American continent, along its waterways and coasts. An adult North American river otter can weigh between 11.0 and 30.9 lb. he river otter is protected and insulated by a thick, water-repellent coat of fur.
